Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a pre-engineered building take to construct?

On average, a standard pre-engineered building takes 4 to 8 weeks to fabricate and erect, significantly faster than traditional concrete structures. The exact timeline depends on the project's size and complexity.

Are porta cabins durable and weather-resistant?

Yes, all our porta cabins and office containers are built with high-quality steel and superior insulation, making them highly durable and capable of withstanding extreme weather conditions.

Do you offer customized designs?

Absolutely. Our team of expert engineers and architects will work closely with you to design a customized solution tailored specifically to your operational requirements and site constraints.

What areas do you serve?

While we are headquartered in Lahore, Build Zone Prefab provides construction and delivery services across Pakistan, executing large-scale industrial projects nationwide.
